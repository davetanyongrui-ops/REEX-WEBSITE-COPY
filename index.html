About REEX

At REEX, we are more than just a property agency. We are a team of forward thinking Real Estate Consultants collectively driven by professionalism, precision and purpose. Strategically based in Riau Islands - Batam, Indonesia, REEX is uniquely positioned at the crossroads of opportunity, leveraging regional growth in Singapore, Indonesia and Malaysia while upholding the highest standards of integrity and service.

Wholly owned by SRP Indonesia (Sinar Rimba Persada group), what sets REEX apart is our distinguished board of advisors, comprising Singapore licensed real estate agents and seasoned investors. Their deep expertise and proven track record guide our operations, allowing us to seamlessly integrate the Singapore Professional Standard and Code of Ethics into every facet of our business. This ensures that every transaction, from property consultation to closing, is handled with transparency, accountability and exceptional care.

By blending local market knowledge with Singapore's renowned professionalism, REEX delivers a unique standard of service that inspires confidence and drives results.

Whether you are a first time buyer, seasoned investor or international stakeholder looking to tap into the region's growing real estate potential, with REEX, you're not just making a property decision, but a smart, ethical and forward looking investment because with our focused approach on quality over quantity, each client will receive our personalized and insightful guidance and attention.
