What We Do

Comprehensive real estate solutions tailored for cross-border investors

1

Cross-Border Investment Advisory

We guide clients through the comparative legal, regulatory, taxation and cultural landscapes of property investment in different jurisdictions - Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia (Johor, Kuala Lumpur and Selangor) as well as UAE (Dubai). From minimum thresholds for foreign buyers in Johor to ownership rules and visa or permit implications, we provide clarity so you can invest with confidence.

2

Tailored Property Scouting & Portfolio Matching

Every investor has unique criteria for risk tolerance, investment horizon, preferred asset type (residential, commercial, mixed-used and industrial), and return of investment expectations. We specialize in matching those with curated listings in Batam and greater Jakarta, Singapore, Johor, Kuala Lumpur, Selangor or Dubai, ensuring balanced between appreciation potential rental yield and ease of management.

3

Due Diligence & Transaction Management

We ensure transparency & integrity on land/title verification, compliance checks, structuring of deals, cross-border payment logistics, legal assistance and local taxation matters, together with our consortium of professional advisors and partners. For example, foreign property investors planning to purchase landed properties in Singapore will require state consent while in Batam, Johor and Kuala Lumpur, recent regulatory shifts have eased foreign ownership thresholds and clarified registration, which we keep abreast of.

4

After-Sales Service & Asset Management

The process of purchasing and owning real estate is one thing. Optimizing the investment is another. We provide property management, tenant sourcing, maintenance oversight and regular performance reporting. If you are abroad (in Singapore, Malaysia Dubai or elsewhere), we act as your local representative to protect your investment.

5

Market Insights & Strategic Forecasts

At REEX, our real estate consultants don't just show properties. We deliver key market information and intelligence such as what is driving demand (eg. Singapore and Malaysian investors in Johor, foreign interest in Batam's coastal, SEZ and mixed-use zones), what infrastructure or policy changes may affect your investments and where the "next-hot" submarkets are (eg. the first Jakarta - Bandung high speed rail as well as the second Jakarta - Surabaya high speed rail currently under negotiation).
